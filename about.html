About CGPA Calculator

Welcome to our CGPA Calculator, a powerful and user-friendly tool designed to help students accurately calculate their Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA).
Whether you are a student in any University, Polytechnic, College or pursuing higher education, our calculator simplifies the process of tracking your academic performance.

What is CGPA?

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is a measure of a student's overall academic performance across all semesters.
It is an important metric used by educational institutions to assess a student's progress and overall achievement.

How to Use the CGPA Calculator

For example: Kunle took these four courses and his course units, grades and points are recorded below for a semester.

S/N Course Code Course unit Course Grade Course Grade Point
1 MTH101 5 A 5
2 PHY101 2 B 4
3 BOT101 1 C 3
4 CHM101 4 A 5
  • Grade A = 5points, Grade B = 4points, Grade C = 3points, Grade D = 2points, Grade E = 1point, Grade F = 0point
  • Kunle's course units = 5, 2, 1, 4
  • Kunle's course grade points = 5, 4, 3, 5
  • Kunle's GPA is 4.67
